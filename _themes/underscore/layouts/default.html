Eggs and Dairy


Eggs - Great American Egg is a family owned farm in Powell Butte, Oregon that has the highest standards for raising animals. We are proud and honored to be able to use their eggs in our dishes.

Dairy - Eberhards Dairy is a family owned business has been supplying central Oregon with dairy products since 1951. They work only with sustainable farms that refuse the use of growth hormones.

Pork


Calrton farms has been crafting natural meats in the northwest for over 50 years. They hand pick all of their animals which means that all of their animals must be raised and handled in a humane manner. They have had Dr. Temple Grandin, the foremost advocate for humane animal handling, review their facility to ensure that they comply with her ideals and guidelines. See http://grandin.com

Chicken


Draper Valley Farms is the pioneer of Washington and Oregon-raised fresh chicken since 1935. They do not use any antibiotics, hormones or preservatives and have an audit trail for each flock.

Flour/Wheat


Shepherds grain is basically the definition of sustainable farming. Quality farming practices result in the best flour possible. We can trace every bag to the farm in which it was grown.

Beef


The ranchers at Country Natural Beef are true stewards of the land. They are focused on the sustainability of their families and their communities. Founded on the principal of providing their customers with healthy and wholesome products. Thanks!
